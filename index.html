Clear, transparent information about our fees and Medicare arrangements

Yes — Medicare rebates are processed automatically on the day of your consultation. Standard consultations attract a $43.90 rebate, while longer consultations (>20 minutes) attract an $84.90 rebate.
No additional charge applies for FotoFinder digital dermoscopic imaging with Dr Reiham Ibrahim. The standard consultation fee covers this advanced imaging technology.
We do not offer DVA direct-billing for skin cancer consultations or procedures. DVA patients will need to pay out-of-pocket fees and seek reimbursement directly from DVA.
