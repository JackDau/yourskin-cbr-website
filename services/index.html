Our Services

Comprehensive skin cancer services provided by our team of specialist doctors.

Full Body Skin Checks

Comprehensive examination of your entire body to detect skin cancers early.

  • Complete head-to-toe examination
  • Dermoscopy for detailed mole analysis
  • Digital photography for monitoring
  • Immediate results and recommendations
Duration: 30-45 minutes

Dermoscopy

Advanced magnification technology for precise skin lesion analysis.

  • 10x magnification examination
  • Pattern analysis of suspicious lesions
  • Real-time assessment
  • Enhanced diagnostic accuracy
Duration: 15-30 minutes

Skin Cancer Excision

Expert surgical removal of skin cancers with minimal scarring.

  • Local anaesthetic procedures
  • Precise surgical techniques
  • Tissue sent for pathology
  • Post-operative care included
Duration: 30-60 minutes

Skin Biopsies

Tissue sampling for definitive diagnosis of suspicious lesions.

  • Punch biopsy technique
  • Shave biopsy options
  • Minimal discomfort
  • Quick healing process
Duration: 15-20 minutes

Skin Cancer Surveillance

Regular monitoring for patients with history of skin cancer.

  • Scheduled follow-up appointments
  • Progress monitoring
  • Early detection protocols
  • Personalised surveillance plans
Duration: 20-30 minutes

Minor Procedures

Treatment of various benign skin conditions and cosmetic concerns.

  • Mole removal
  • Skin tag removal
  • Cyst removal
  • Cryotherapy treatments
Duration: 15-45 minutes
