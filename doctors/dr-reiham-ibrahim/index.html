About Dr Reiham Ibrahim

Dr Reiham Ibrahim is a highly experienced GP with a special passion for detecting and treating skin cancer early—often before it becomes life-threatening. With over 25 years in medicine, she has dedicated much of her career to saving lives through early skin cancer diagnosis.

Dr Ibrahim holds advanced training in skin cancer detection, diagnosis, and surgical treatment. Using cutting-edge video dermoscopy technology that magnifies skin structures up to 400x, she can spot changes as small as a few millimetres—well before they're visible to the naked eye. Her precision and thorough approach give patients confidence and peace of mind.

Soon to be an Accredited Skin Cancer Doctor with the Skin Cancer College Australasia, Dr Ibrahim has removed thousands of lesions and provides expert care for both simple and complex skin cancers at our dedicated Skin Clinic. Whether you're booking a routine skin check or need expert care for a suspicious spot, you're in exceptionally capable hands with Dr Reiham Ibrahim.
