About Dr Kit Wong

Dr Kit Wong graduated with honours from the University of Sydney in 2006 and is a Fellow of the RACGP. He completed his Masters of Medicine (Skin Cancer) from the University of Queensland in 2018, bringing advanced expertise to skin cancer diagnosis and treatment.

With over 10 years of experience in Skin Cancer Medicine, Dr Wong is actively involved in educating GP registrars through the college training program. He mentors and educates medical students and other GPs in the field, demonstrating his commitment to advancing skin cancer care.
